Background

My journey into holistic medicine began over a decade ago when I was first employed by a naturopathic medical provider as a front office administrator. At that time my life changed in the direction of Eastern medicine.

Since those early career moments, I have enrolled in massage therapy school, worked as a co-founder for a behavioral health startup, and most recently was employed as a front office administrator for an acupuncturist office.

I believe myself to be sensitive to softer, inner, energies, and that sensitivity has pushed me in the direction of biodynamic cranial sacral therapy. It has become my passion. I am currently six months away from completing a three year certification program, and I would be grateful for the opportunity to develop my practice with you.

Approach

Practice Philosophy

My intent is to create an environment where you feel comfortable, safe, and welcomed. During sessions, we will always move at a pace where you feel comfortable. I make it a point to always ask before moving forward with any tactile touch. I periodically check in with patients throughout sessions.

Sessions are held at my home. Before and after each session, I wipe down common areas. To prepare the session room, I typically burn palo santos, sweetgrass, or non-scented candles (depending on your preference) before each session.

If there are any special requests to make you feel more comfortable or safe, I will do my best to accommodate them.
